INPUT Mission Specifications

Initial Sizing: Dimensions, Weight and Balance
WHILE Design is Not Functional and Not Optimal DO
    IF First Iteration THEN
        Use: Initial Sizing
    ELSE
        Adjust: Sizing from Previous Iteration
    ENDIF
        Design: Aircraft Geometry

        Design and Analyze: Power Generation
        Design: Structure of Lifting Surfaces
        Design: Fuselage Layout and Structure
        Evaluate Design
    
ENDWHILE


Develop: Guidance, Navigation and Control System

OUTPUT Aircraft
